Attend the Diallo Tour

About

The Diallo's - consisting of Fernanda, Travis, Ace, Holly, and Sam - is a family of five who are currently ranked the #3 popular stand-up comedians.

The Diallo's started performing at subway stations. They continued to do so for eight whole years. Later, the Diallo's began to gain more and more attendees, slowly increasing their popularity. The Diallo's moved to small bars, then little stages and finally, the big stages.

Many attendees enjoy their lovable comedy. One of the attendees concluded, "The Diallo's is a funny group of people. I love their jokes, their point of views, and imitations of other people. I also love the way they connect together." The attendee then concluded, "If you enjoy comedy, you should definitely attend one of their events."

The Diallo's are currently hosting their third annual tour. One special guest, Kevin Hart - the current #1 comedian - will be joining the Diallo's in their tour. Other guests include Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson, Jack Black, Adam Sandler, and many more. An enormous number of people registered and are excited for the event. All agreed that this tour is going to be "one of the best tours yet."
